Job.IT

IT Outsource has a direct relationship with client companies because it is about the workers. In our scheme of services, IT Outsourcing is the practical one. It is handling for the human resources that contracted with company clients to do something, in this case, IT Project. IT Outsource can take almost all IT works. Some of Big Division in IT Outsource is Web Development, Software Development, Hardware Engineering, IT Planner, Database/Server Maintenance, Network Management, and many more.

Ask.IT

IT Consultant is the "gate" of the IT Outsource department. At first glance, there is no significant difference between IT Outsource and IT Consultant because both are handling the same IT works. The difference between IT Outsourcing and IT Consulting is the process. IT Outsourcing takes part in Practical of the Project, different from IT Consultant that takes roles in Discussion and Planning Process before doing a project.

Edu.IT

IT Courses will take part in the educational field of IT. In IT Education, the curriculum will fastly change to keep in touch with the latest technology developments. In this case, it also has a relationship with Industrials demand for Human resource quality that keeps in touch with the latest technology developments. IT Courses is the implementation of this problem solution. This department will be responsible for preparing and training people interested in IT and have chosen IT as their career path. IT Courses will provide an IT education that keeps in touch with the industrial demand, which will increase the opportunities of the students to be accepted in a company.
